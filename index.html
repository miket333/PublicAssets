📊 How Credits Work

1 Credit = 1 City Normalized

Each city name you process consumes one credit from your monthly quota. Batch operations are charged per city in the batch.

  • Single Search: Normalizing "Nw Yrk" → "New York, NY, United States" = 1 credit
  • Batch Normalize: Processing 100 cities in one request = 100 credits
  • Lookup by ID: Retrieving a city by its unique ID = 1 credit

Credits reset at the beginning of each billing cycle. Unused credits do not roll over.

🚀 Key Features

  • Fuzzy Matching: Handles typos like "Nw Yrk", "Los Anglese", "Sinsinati"
  • Abbreviation Expansion: Understands "NYC" → New York, "LA" → Los Angeles
  • Global Coverage: Cities worldwide
  • Fast Response: Sub-100ms average response time
  • Country/State Filtering: Narrow searches by country or state code
  • Fallback Results: Returns multiple matches when confidence is low
